Best Hair Supplements

Hair loss is a common problem that affects both men and women, generally when they start aging. But don’t worry, this can be managed with a balanced intake of essential nutrients which encourages hair growth, and reduces the chance of hair problems due to nutrient deficiencies. 

Supplements for hair growth contain all the essential nutrients and potent ingredients, promoting hair growth and thickness. These are designed for men, women, and both genders, to help achieve the best results.

Importance of Hair Supplements

A balanced consisting of all the macros and micronutrients (vitamin C, D, Biotin, Folic acid, and many more) is the best way to improve hair health. If your body lacks these nutrients, it can directly affect hair quality, growth rate, and volume.

However, it is not possible every time to include all the nutrients in your plate, and that’s where hair growth supplements become essential in promoting hair health.

These hair supplements for hair growth have nutrients like vitamin D, iron, and vitamin C which support the hair growth cycle, follicle strength, and scalp health.  

Types of Hair Supplements for Healthy Hair

Omega-3:

Omega-3 fatty acid provides a shine to the hair, prevents dryness, and maintains a flake-free scalp. Hence, to attain all these benefits, you must include a recommended dosage of 600mg of omega-3 supplement in your daily lifestyle. 

Vitamin C:

Vitamin C is not only good for immunity but also plays a major role in hair growth and helps fight dandruff while also preventing hair loss and split ends. Additionally, it improves blood circulation to the scalp, stimulating hair follicles.

Vitamin D:

If you are someone deficient in vitamin D, this could be the reason for your hair loss. Vitamin D is responsible for hair follicle growth and supports healthy keratinocytes, which produce keratin for hair growth.

B Complex:

B vitamins, especially B7 (biotin) and B12 are popularly known for hair health, strengthening, and conditioning. Especially biotin (B7) vitamin, which helps in red blood cell production and delivers oxygen, and nutrition to the scalp. 

Saw Palmetto:

Saw palmetto is a herbal supplement for hair growth that combats hair loss in men and women significantly. In research, it is found that saw palmetto blocks the enzyme that converts testosterone to DHT and reduces hair loss.

Ashwagandha:

Ashwagandha helps in regrowth of hair follicles and strengthens them, thereby reducing scalp hair loss. Additionally, it can stop hair loss associated with stress.

Shatavari:

Shatavari aka "Queen of herbs, promotes hair growth, soothes the scalp, and prevents irritation and flakiness. It also helps in strengthening hair roots and maintains the hair's natural color, preventing premature greying.

Zinc:

Zinc is equally important for hair health. It is observed that a person with zinc deficiency experiences faster hair loss, which makes this mineral essential for maintaining healthy hair. 

Collagen:

Collagen is a protein, that acts as a building material for hair structure. Hence, including collagen supplements in your diet can increase the body's hair-building proteins, resulting in longer and thicker hair.

Iron:

Iron is one of the important minerals for overall health, it carries oxygen to the body's tissues, including the scalp. If a person is deficient in Iron which is more common in women, it can lead to anemia and hair loss due to inadequate oxygen delivery to the hair follicles.

Coconut Oil:

Coconut oil is an effective pre-wash and post-wash treatment for nourishing damaged hair follicles and hair shafts. 

Health Benefits of Hair Supplements

There are various benefits of hair supplements for individuals experiencing hair loss, and seeking to improve their hair health:

1. Promotes Keratin Production: These supplements are formulated with essential nutrients like biotin, vitamins, and minerals that support keratin production, the key protein responsible for hair strength and growth.

2. Prevents Hair fall: These supplements also help strengthen the hair shaft and prevent breakage, leading to thicker and fuller-looking hair.

3. Fulfills Nutritional Deficiency: Most of the time, the result of hair loss is due to improper nutrition or nutritional deficiencies. Through hair growth supplements you can provide proper nutrition to hair follicles, and ensure that the body receives adequate nutrients necessary for healthy hair.

4. Strengthening of Hair Follicles: Helps in the creation of new hair follicles and supports the immune system. Low levels of vitamin D have been linked to hair shedding and thinning.

5. Regulates Oxygen levels to Hair Roots:  Iron enables oxygen delivery to the hair roots, which is essential for hair growth. A deficiency can lead to anemia-related hair loss.
